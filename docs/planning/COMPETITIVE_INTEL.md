# Competitive Intelligence — Spokane Mobile Mechanic Market

_Audited March 2026_

## Primary Competitor: Sypher's Mobile Mechanic

**Website:** syphersmobilemechanic.com
**Owner:** Donald Sypher
**Phone:** (509) 218-0157

### Pricing
- Domestic vehicles: $120/hr
- European vehicles: $160/hr
- Diesel trucks: $200/hr
- Diagnostic inspection: $150 (credited toward approved repairs)
- Travel charges: $0 within 10mi of downtown, $15 Valley, $25 Liberty Lake, $40 max

### Strengths
- ASE certification prominently featured
- European vehicle specialization (BMW, Mercedes, Audi, Volvo)
- Published transparent pricing page with tiered rates
- Personal story/brand narrative (15+ years, single father, community focus)
- Same-day emergency repair positioning
- Accepts Visa/MC/Amex/Apple Pay/Google Pay via Stripe
- Diesel fleet services (commercial segment)

### Weaknesses (Wrench Ready advantages)
- **SEO:** No neighborhood-level pages; limited to city-wide claims
- **Schema:** No FAQ schema, minimal structured data
- **Service pages:** Generic — not optimized per service type
- **Design:** Functional but not modern; lacks visual hierarchy
- **No diagnostic tool:** No self-service symptom checker
- **No form backend:** Phone/text contact only
- **Content depth:** Service descriptions are thin; limited FAQ content
- **No Google Ads landing pages:** All traffic goes to homepage

### Services NOT Offered (potential WR differentiation)
- Tire installation/repair
- Engine/transmission removal & rebuild
- Lockout services

4. **Owner story:** No Simon introduction page (Sypher's has a detailed bio)
5. **Published hourly rates:** WR shows "From $X" per service but no hourly breakdown
